# SEO Blog Writer Skill

## Core Design Principles
*   **Verify Before Assert:** Never include a statistic, quote, or claim without verifying it via search.
*   **Minimum Viable Input:** Start with just a **Topic** and **Domain**.
*   **Honest Degradation:** If a tool or search fails, state it clearly and move to the next best step rather than hallucinating.
*   **Incremental Execution:** Write and verify section-by-section to ensure quality and prevent loss of work.

## The 6-Phase Workflow

### Phase 0: Context Discovery (Automated Research)
1.  **Product Intelligence:** Extract Product/Service Name, Value Proposition, and Brand Voice from the portfolio.
2.  **Keyword Intelligence:** Identify Primary and Secondary Keywords based on search intent.
3.  **Competitive Intelligence:** Analyze top URLs for Content Gaps.

### Phase 0.5: The Confirmation Gate (Mandatory)
Summary for approval: Target Keyword, Proposed H1 & Word Count, Competitor Gaps, Internal Links.

### Phase 1: Research Brief
Assemble `research-brief.md` with H1 optimization (under 60 chars), H2 structure (Problem -> Solution -> Steps -> FAQ), and linking strategy.

### Phase 2: Content Generation (The Writing)
*   **Opening:** Keyword in first 100 words; direct answer to intent.
*   **Body Standards:** Max 3-4 sentences per paragraph. Vary sentence lengths.
*   **GEO Optimization:** Use "Evidence Sandwiches" (Claim -> Evidence -> Interpretation) and Direct Answer Blocks (40-60 words).
*   **Linking:** Natural link insertion.

### Phase 3: AI Pattern Prevention (Humanizing)
*   **Banned Words:** Remove: delve, tapestry, seamlessly, robust, leverage, harness, elevate, revolutionize, paradigm, synergy.
*   **Banned Phrases:** Avoid: "In today's fast-paced world," "It's no secret that," "In conclusion."
*   **The "AI-Tell" Check:** Scan for em-dash overuse, rhetorical questions as transitions, and triple-adjective stacking.

### Phase 4: Quality Assurance (QA)
*   Scoring (0-100): SEO (40), Content (30), Linking (20), Brand (10).
*   Factual Check against official documentation.

### Phase 5: Delivery Package
Finalize with Frontmatter, Schema Markup (JSON-LD), and Promotion Checklist.

## Writing Standards
*   **Readability:** Flesch Reading Ease score of 60-70.
*   **Voice:** Energy (4/5), Formality (3/5).
*   **Authenticity:** Read-Aloud Test.
